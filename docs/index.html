"Transforming Science through Data-driven Discovery"

CyVerse transcends traditional computing projects to fill a niche created by the computing era. Technology advancements have led to a new era of using diverse, distributed datasets to test larger, more complex hypotheses. Our powerful, extensible platforms provide computational infrastructure to handle massive datasets and large-scale analyses. We empower global collaboration to enable data-driven discovery.

Connect

Bring your dataset or computational problem, study publicly accessible data hosted in our data commons, or federate with our resources to streamline your project. All available at the touch of a keypad from anywhere in the world.

Learn More
Analyze

Manage your data with our suite of platforms for secure storage and analysis, or integrate your own tools. CyVerse provides high-throughput computing allocations and cloud-based virtual machines so you can customize your computation environment.

Learn More
Collaborate

Give colleagues around the world specialized access to your dataset or project, or make your completed dataset publicly accessible, track-able with a unique digital object identifier. Empower other researchers or students with our educational curricula.
